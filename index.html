Head of Sustainability and High School Senior @ Tanglin Trust School, Singapore

WHO AM I?

Innovator, Pioneer, Leader, Changemaker

With a flair for Mathematics and Physics, an ability to harness technology, a bond with the Humanities to understand the social impact of my work and an unrelenting dedication to my passions, I pride myself on having the tools and the drive to solve problems which run rampant in today's world. I've already commenced jet propulsion on this trajectory, working on two projects to make Math accessible to all types of learners, developing countless sustainable technological solutions and diving into the worlds of cyber security and quantum mechanics. I'm so deeply entrenched in research and innovation that the best I can do now is hope that I don't turn into that unsuspecting academician in a sci-fi movie who ends up creating a horde of malevolent chimeras, an army of self-aware robots or an Earth-swallowing black hole.

When I'm not pushing the boundaries of human knowledge, you can find me launching into monologues on stage, crafting nifty arguments in debate, hovering over the buzzer on trivia night or delivering my deadly spinning serve in table tennis. 


Don't hesitate to contact me if any of this sounds even remotely interesting to you!
